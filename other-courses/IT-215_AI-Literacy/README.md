# IT-215: AI Literacy

## Course Description

This course provides a foundational understanding of artificial intelligence technologies, their capabilities, limitations, and practical applications in business and technology contexts. Students explore AI concepts, learn effective prompt engineering techniques, and analyze real-world AI implementations. The course culminates in a comprehensive project applying AI solutions to call center operations, demonstrating how AI can transform business processes and customer service.

## Key Topics

- **AI Fundamentals**: Machine learning, natural language processing, and neural networks
- **Prompt Engineering**: Crafting effective prompts for AI systems and large language models
- **AI Capabilities**: Understanding what AI can and cannot do effectively
- **AI Applications**: Real-world use cases across industries
- **Generative AI**: Text generation, content creation, and conversational AI
- **Ethical AI**: Bias, fairness, transparency, and responsible AI deployment
- **Business Integration**: Implementing AI solutions in organizational contexts
- **AI Limitations**: Understanding boundaries and potential pitfalls

## Tools & Technologies

- **AI Platforms**: Large Language Models (LLMs) and generative AI tools
- **Prompt Engineering**: Techniques for effective AI interaction
- **Documentation**: Professional business case development
- **Analysis Tools**: Evaluating AI capabilities and limitations
- **Case Studies**: Real-world AI implementation examples
